My name is **Jikai Wang** *(Wahng Jee-kai, 王继凯)*. I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the [University of Texas at Dallas](https://www.utdallas.edu/), working in the [Intelligent Robotics and Vision Lab](https://labs.utdallas.edu/irvl/) under the guidance of Prof. [Yu Xiang](https://yuxng.github.io/) and co-advised by Prof. [Xiaohu Guo](https://personal.utdallas.edu/~xguo/).

My research focuses on **robotic manipulation and perception**, with an emphasis on **3D reconstruction, 6D pose estimation, and hand-object interaction for dexterous manipulation**. I develop **robust, scalable, and deployable systems** that bridge perception and real-world robotic applications.

Prior to my Ph.D., I worked as a [Senior Engineer](https://www.ericsson.com/en/careers/engineering) at [Ericsson](https://www.ericsson.com/en) (China), contributing to **4G/LTE core network design, system integration, and large-scale deployment**.

I hold an [M.S. in Computer Science](https://cs.utdallas.edu/) from [UT Dallas](https://www.utdallas.edu/) and a [B.S. in Communications Engineering](https://seie.hit.edu.cn/english/)  from [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://en.hit.edu.cn/) (HIT).

**Interests:** Robotics • Computer Vision • AI • Embodied Intelligence
